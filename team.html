Our Team

Jeffrey Dietrich Ph.D.

Fascinated by the capabilities of natural systems, Jeffrey has spent his career developing biological solutions to pressing societal and environmental challenges.

He was Co-Founder and CTO at Lygos from 2011-2019, where he led the R&D team in scaling a clean, low-cost biochemical manufacturing process.

He earned his Ph.D. in BioEngineering from UC-Berkeley in 2011, where his graduate research involved protein engineering to produce artemisinin, an antimalarial drug.
